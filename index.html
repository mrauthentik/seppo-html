ABOUT US

WHO WE ARE

When Fashion Matters
Creativity is Needed.

OgO Oluwa fashion house is a one-stop Nigerian fashion shop for the making of trending Nigerian fashion attires as well popular traditional wears, for all ages and genders. we also offer a list of tailoring services, ranging from construction to altering, modifying and repairing of apparel to the needs, specifications and preferences of individuals in Nigeria. OgO Oluwa fashion house also doubles as a training institute for individuals interested in learning the fine art of tailoring!

OUR MISSION

We exist to bring to life, trending Nigerian fashion in either its trending fashion styles or traditional style. We make easily accessible, popular Nigerian cultural attires and latest fashion nationwide. We modify the designs and styles of attires to the preferred fit and design of customers.

OUR VISION

To make known, the diversity of the world of fashion and attires that Nigeria is home to, through our gallery of trendy fashion outfits and traditional styles from cultures in Nigeria. To increase the level of inter-cultural interaction and harmony through the use of different cultural attires by individuals across the country. To provide Nigerians with the opportunity to experience and live the beautiful of cultures of our fatherland, through clothes. Become a proud owner of a piece (or lots) of Nigeria's trendiest fashion picks by simply selecting and placing your order HERE.
