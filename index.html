ABOUT US

WHO WE ARE

When Fashion Matters
Creativity is Needed.

OgO Oluwa fashion house is a non-stop Nigerian fashion shop for the sewing of high-quality but affordable trending Nigerian Fashion Attires, as well popular traditional wears, for all ages and genders.
Founded by Ogoluwa Mathew whom it's named after, the fashion house started out as a regular tailoring shop with a strong desire for quality. And that strong intent became the foundation of our growth today.
Knowing that a large number of tailors frequently use attractive but actually inferior materials on high-quality and expensive customer fabrics, OgO_Oluwa fashion house aims at doing the exact opposite!.
Here at OgO_Oluwa fashion house, we put our reputation on the line for each and every of our clothes, due to our principle of using only the best quality and ethically-sourced materials.
But that's not all, we also offer a list of tailoring services ranging from sewing to altering, modifying and reparing of clothes, designs and sizes to meet your desired specifications and needs.
Our expertise has earned us the Standard of doubting as a training institute for individuals interested in being trained or furthering their skills in the fine art of tailoring!.

OUR MISSION

We exist to bring to life, trending Nigerian fashion in latest or traditional.
We make easily accessible, popular Nigerian cultural attires and lastest fashion picks nationwide through our quicks delivery.
We alter the designs and stles of attires to fit the style and preference of customers.

OUR VISION

To make known, the diversity of the world of fashion and attires that Nigeria is home to, through our gallery of trendy fashion outfits and traditional styles from cultures across Nigeria.
To increase the level of inter-cultural interaction and harmony through the use of different cultural attires by individuals across the country. To provide Nigerians with an opportunity to live and experience the beautiful shades and cultures of fashion.
